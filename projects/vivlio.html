Vivlio

A minimalist digital bookshelf

Client

Self-Project

Role

Design and Development

Platforms

Android, Website

A time-tracking tool, so you can better organize your objectives.

Free and OpenSource.

Vivlio is a minimalist digital bookshelf designed to help you organize your books by category, author and genre.

Vivlio can be used as a daily essential for people of all ages who love to read or even for those who hate to read.

Design and built by Héctor Olivares
